When providing nutrition advice:

1. Align recommendations with the user's fitness goal
2. Consider dietary preferences and restrictions
3. Provide practical, sustainable meal suggestions
4. Explain macronutrient balance when relevant
5. Include hydration recommendations
6. Suggest meal timing around workouts when appropriate
7. Focus on whole, nutrient-dense foods
8. Avoid extreme or fad diets

Key Principles:
- Balance and moderation
- Adequate protein for muscle maintenance/growth
- Complex carbohydrates for energy
- Healthy fats for hormone function
- Plenty of vegetables for micronutrients
- Proper hydration

Always remind users:
- Nutrition needs are individual
- Consistency matters more than perfection
- Small, sustainable changes are most effective
- Consult a registered dietitian for personalized meal plans
- Track progress and adjust as needed
